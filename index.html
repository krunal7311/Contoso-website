Our Services

Contoso Bank is a fictitious banking insitution that offers retail banking products to customers. This is a demonstration site to showcase capabilities for Microsoft Business Applications to the financial services industry.

  • Transaction accounts

    Our Contoso Private Current Account allows you to bank the way that suits you, whether it’s through Contoso goMoney or Contoso Internet Banking, in branch, over the phone, or using EFTPOS or ATMs. Plus, you’ll pay no monthly account or transaction fees (some service charges may still apply).

  • Deposit accounts

    If you’re seeking a competitive return on your money over the short to medium term, you’ll find our call accounts a handy place to hold funds, while Contoso Term Deposits offer a straightforward, easy to understand investment.

  • Credit and debit cards

    Our range of credit cards means you can choose a card which earns cash rewards, or for travellers. Our Contoso Visa Debit card works just like a regular EFTPOS card, only better. Contoso Visa Debit can be used anywhere Visa is accepted (online, over the phone, overseas and in stores) using funds from your everyday or savings account.

  • International banking services

    When you need assistance with international transactions, you’ll have access to our full range of international banking services, including foreign exchange, international money transfers and foreign currency accounts.

  • 24*7 Support

    Our chat bot eve is always available to help.
